Voice His People

Amplifying the voices of the Great Commission

Our mission is simple, to support Godly ordained ministry in the complex world of A/V systems, helping churches document, understand, and maintain their systems.

The daily driver of many churches is Tribal Knowledge, depending on one "tech guy" to have the technical know-how. Often, once churches loose their one tech guy, they don't have the knowledge to use their own systems anymore. Voice His People seeks to help churches avoid this growing issue, by making A/V accessible to everyone.
We are happy to work with you to author custom guides and documents, keep them safe and secure, and ensure they are available to all staff.
